Mars Rover Timeline

Curiosity Rover

Launch: November 26, 2011

Landing: August 6, 2012

Curiosity confirmed that ancient Mars had the conditions necessary to support microbial life. It continues to explore Gale Crater and study Martian rock layers.

Perseverance Rover

Launch: July 30, 2020

Landing: February 18, 2021

Perseverance is collecting rock cores, exploring the Jezero Crater delta, and studying past habitability while testing technologies for future human missions.
