Mars Rover Timeline

Curiosity Rover

Launch: November 26, 2011

Landing: August 6, 2012

Curiosity’s mission confirmed ancient Mars had the conditions needed to support microbial life. It continues exploring Gale Crater’s layered rocks and mountain formations.

Perseverance Rover

Launch: July 30, 2020

Landing: February 18, 2021

Equipped with advanced instruments, Perseverance investigates ancient environments and collects core samples for future return missions.
